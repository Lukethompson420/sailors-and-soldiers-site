Welcome

Welcome to the historic Steeton Sailors & Soldiers Club, proudly serving our community since 1919. Whether you're here for good company, entertainment, or a pint, you're among friends.

About Us

Founded in the wake of the First World War, our club is a proud home for servicemen, veterans, and civilians alike. We host events, community nights, and more — all in the spirit of camaraderie and remembrance.
